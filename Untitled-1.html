1.1  Introduction and document purpose

Recreational Amalgamated Holdings (RAH) was formed by a collection of venture capitalist in the year of 2013 for the easy management of large-scale health and holiday resorts around Australia. RAH grew rapidly and made various acquisitions through out time and now include a group of companies under its umbrella namingly Pacific Resorts Group, Snowy Mountains Resorts Group, Family Fitness Group, Southern Golf Group and Vista Events Group.

 

Vista events has been involved in organising small to medium scale events such as club tours, speaking seminars and small scale entertainment. However it now plans to organise more significant and larger engagements to drive its business growth. However in order to do so they are restricted by not having a sufficient information infrastructure thus decided to implement a fully integrated event management system in order enable them to deal with such large scale events by automating management processes currently requiring heavy human intervention.

 

The purpose of this project management briefing paper is to outline the development plan of the event management system.  The document initially focuses on areas of stakeholder identification, recommendation of development approach, communication needs, risk management strategy, quality management and project monitoring and control strategies.

 

Next it moves on to analyse the preliminary project management activities such as high level work breakdown structure, task sequencing based on activity-on-node (AON), AON PERT, key milestones of the project, time management and budgeting.

 

The document concludes by providing recommendations and suggested next steps in order get the project underway.

1.2 Overview of Events Management System (EMS)

The main goal of the EMS is to provide Vista group with easy running of significant entertainment events by automating management processes currently requiring heavy human intervention.

 

In order for the system to be successful it should encompass objectives such as maximising event attendance, ticketing and registration, venue management supplier management, performance management, performer booking, budget management, sponsorship management and data analytics.

 

Another core success criteria of the system should be the ability for the system to be integrated with the rest of the RAH as the parent company has decided to update the information infrastructure across all its companies. Hence the architecture of the EMS plays a pivotal role in order for it to support characteristics such as scalability, maintainability and modifiability.

 

In order for the EMS to be successful it is also necessary for the system to be fully developed and implemented by January 30th 2017. This is an extreme tight schedule considering the magnitude of this system.  However, not being able to meet this deadline  will result in project failure it will have major repercussions on Vista events who has already won a contract to manage a major event for January 2017.

 

1.3 Measurable Organisational Value (MOV)

The measurable organisational value (MOV) is the projects success and overall goal. Projects should alight with the company?s overall strategy.

 

1.3.1 Desired areas of impact

Below are the desired potential areas of impact by execution of the EMS project.

 

1.3 1.1 Strategic

Vista has traditionally operated as management company for cub tours, speaking seminars and small scale entertainments by overseas performers. They are planning on bidding for more significant engagements, which includes Australian tours by international artists and the management of large-scale concerts, a strategic move to penetrate new markets and increase market share.

 

At present Vista does not have a fully integrated event management system in order to deal with such events and the implementation f EMS will enable Vista to handle the complex nature of such large scale events helping them in their strategic moves.

 

1.3.1.2 Operational

One of the goals of Vista is to get rid of their old equipment and replace them with new technology in order to streamline internal operations. Currently their management processes require heavy human intervention for organising events. Vista wants to try and automate these processes as much as possible gearing them to cope with events of much larger magnitude.

 

Therefore, the EMS would automate much of this manual work resulting in lowered costs due to streamlined operations, increased operational effectiveness and better management of supply chain.

 

1.3.3 Financial

Enabling the management of complex event by means of implementing a fully integrated EMS will also result in more business revenue and thus increased profits.

1.3.2 Desired value of the IT project

Implementing the EMS drives the value in the following aspects.

 

1.3.2.1 Better

Once the EMS is implemented, it will not only help organise more complex and large scale events, it will also help better manage events of all nature. Bringing all information required (venue management, supplier management, performer management, travel itineraries, performance management, sponsorship management, targeted marketing) to manage an event to a single platform makes things more effective and easy to manage.

 

Also utilising historical event data and running data analytics, Vista can manage their future events even better making more effective strategic decisions.

 

1.3.2.2 Faster

By streamlining and automating managing processes, it will not only be able to manage events better but also be possible to manage events faster and in turn more no of events.

 

1.3.3.3 Do more

Empowering employees with all the information in a central location at their fingertips through the EMS will enable their productivity on a daily basis. This will in turn enable Vista to manage more events thus enabling to grow their business and enter new markets.

 

1.3.5  MOV statement

The company?s measurable organisational value is to enable effective and efficient running of large and significant events by automating management processes, streamlining internal operations that currently require heavy human intervention, which in turn will enable Vista to drive its strategy of growth (new markets and increased market share). This will also helps its parent company?s (RAH) goal of development of a suitable integrated infrastructure in order to better monitor and provide services to its chain of affiliates. In order for the project to be successful the EMS has to be fully developed, tested and implemented by 29th January 2017.

 

The EMS should automate the current management processes of event attendance, marketing of an event, ticketing, venue management, easy management of event suppliers, performance management, booking of performers, budget management, sponsorship management and data analytics in order to achieve the afore mentioned MOV.
